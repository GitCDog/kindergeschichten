BUTTON CLICK ORDER
================================================================================

Step 1: 🔄 Scan for New Files
        └─ Auto-detects new stories

Step 2: 📝 Word Count & JSON
        └─ Creates metadata

Step 3: 🎵 Generate Audio
        └─ Converts to MP3

Step 4: 🖼️ Generate Pics
        └─ Creates book covers (enter story numbers)

Step 5: 🎬 Generate Videos
        └─ Creates MP4 + archives JSON & audio

Step 6: ✓ Post
        └─ Move video + mark as posted

================================================================================
TYPICAL TIMING
================================================================================

🔄 Scan:      ~5-10 seconds
📝 JSON:      <1 second per story
🎵 Audio:     10-30 seconds per story
🖼️ Pics:      20-60 seconds per story
🎬 Videos:    5-15 seconds per story
✓ Post:       <1 second per story

TOTAL for 1 story (complete): ~2-3 minutes
